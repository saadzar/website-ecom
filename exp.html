Cooperative Story

Made by women who know the tree, the fruit, and the work behind every drop.

Founded in 2012, the cooperative brings together women from the argan region around shared production, stable income, and long-term stewardship. The process stays close to the source: fruit collection, kernel preparation, slow pressing, and careful bottling in small batches.

“

Argan is not only a product for us. It is a rhythm of work, a local ecosystem, and a source of dignity carried from hand to hand.

From the cooperative notebook
Process

From fruit to bottle,
nothing rushed

The method stays intentionally slow so the oil keeps its natural character, aroma, and value for both care and cuisine.

01

Fruit is gathered

Argan fruit is collected in season and dried carefully before the shells are opened.

02

Kernels are prepared

The hardest part is done by hand, keeping the selected kernels intact and ready for pressing.

03

Oil is cold pressed

A slow mechanical press extracts the oil without unnecessary heat or heavy intervention.

04

Bottles are finished

Each batch is filtered lightly, labeled, and packed for local delivery or export.
